About Dr. Souvik Parbat

Passionate About Dental Health

Dr. Souvik Parbat is a highly skilled and compassionate dental surgeon with over 5 years of experience. He specializes in cosmetic dentistry, dental implants, and oral surgery, dedicated to providing exceptional care to his patients.

After receiving his Doctor of Dental Surgery degree from Calcutta National College & Hospital, Dr. Parbat completed his residency at R. Ahamed Dental College & Hospital, honing his skills in advanced dental procedures.

He is also attached with Ramkrishna Sevashram(Hingalganj) and Experienced Consultant Oral & Dental Surgeon, has degree of B.D.S(Kol) & PGDMH

Dr. Parbat's mission is to transform smiles and improve the overall oral health of his patients. He believes in a patient-centered approach, ensuring that each individual receives personalized care tailored to their unique needs.
